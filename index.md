List comprehensions are a concise way to create lists. 

~~~ {.Python}
lowermost = ['blue', 'green', 'yellow', 'black', 'red'] 
upperlist = []
for colour in lowerlist:
    upperlist.append(colour.upper())
print(upperlist)

~~~

~~~ {.output}
['BLUE', 'GREEN', 'YELLOW', 'BLACK', 'RED']
~~~
